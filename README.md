## üìö Related Theory Course (CSE-425: Digital Signal Processing)

**Credit:** 3.0  
**Course Topics:**
- Discrete-time signals and systems  
- Analog to digital conversion  
- Impulse response (FIR and IIR systems)  
- Difference equation and convolution  
- Transient and steady-state response  
- Discrete Fourier Transform (DFT), Fast Fourier Transform (FFT)  
- Inverse FFT and Z-transformation  
- Transfer function, poles and zeros  
- Circular convolution, auto-correlation, and cross-correlation  
- FIR filters (design using window, optimal, and frequency sampling methods)  
- IIR filters (design using impulse invariant, bilinear Z-transformation)  
- Digital signal processor (TMS family) and DSP applications
